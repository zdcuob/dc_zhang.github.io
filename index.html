About Me


I'm a Associate Professor, at the College of Mechanical Engnieering at Sichuan University. the primary focus lies in the realms of mechanical equipment fault diagnosis, remaining useful life prediction, and health assessment. Before my current position, I worked as an postdoctoral fellow at City University of Hongkong. I completed my PhD at University of Birmingham. My research interests span various areas, including non-destructive testing, signal processing, machine learning, multisensory fusion, and maintenance strategies. I have authored research papers that have garnered over 400 citations on Google Scholar. Some of my work has been published in renowned scientific journals and conferences, where I served as the first author and corresponding author.
